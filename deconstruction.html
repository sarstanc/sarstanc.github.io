PHOTO DECONSTRUCTION

Visual Communication 3 | Professors Michael Beaman and Marla Smith | Fall 2012


Grasshopper definition analyzing RGB, density, and pattern of a photograph. In Rhino, this data is displayed graphically then converted to create 3D forms and manipulated to generate a complete shape. The model is exploded into a file of flat components for lasercutting. Assembled, these pieces form the physical realization of the Rhino-generated model of data.
