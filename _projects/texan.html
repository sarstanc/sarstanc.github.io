Texan

Associate Tech Director -- The Daily Texan

Fall 2013 - Fall 2014

Proposed redesign for The Daily Texan online, the main student news source for The University of Texas at Austin.

The article page mockup was created using HTML/CSS/JS in preparation for a Drupal template to be completed in following semesters. As leader of the design, I worked with an interdisciplinary team of journalism, advertising, and computer science majors.
