GREEN BUILDING CENTER

Design 3 | Professor Sarah Gamble | Fall 2012


Center for promoting green building design and education, located on South Congress Avenue. The design takes influence from trees in the surrounding neighborhood, using tree growth patterns to inform the buildingâ€™s structure. A random grid of columns placed throughout the building provides this structure and generates a meandering path as if traveling through a wooded area. The center has a reduced footprint and open-air first floor that draws visitors through to the rear greenhouse and on-site tree.
