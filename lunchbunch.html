LUNCH BUNCH

IBM | Mobile Design | Summer 2014

Team: Aaron Balderas, Chris Davis, Corbin Montague, Priyanka Sur, Ran Xu


As part of the CIO Mobile Intern team with IBM, I worked with a mixed team of designers and developers to create a mobile food-ordering system from start to finish. Based on personal interviews, we designed the app to ease the process of placing large group meal orders for delivery or pick-up. Our design deliverables for the end of summer included information architecture diagrams, wireframes and use cases, and high-fidelity mockups ready for development.

During the process of creating graphics and developing the initial prototype, we worked with [topcoder], a crowd-sourcing platform, to expedite the app production. Part of my role was to integrate the resulting designs back into our intended style and branding ideas.
