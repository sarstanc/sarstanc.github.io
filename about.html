SARAH STANCIK

is an award-winning classical and jazz pianist, singer, dj and electronic musician, composer, orchestrator, music supervisor, and entrepreneur based in Austin, Texas.

She graduated from the University of Texas at Austin with a degree in music composition and a secondary focus in film. She also studied music production through Berklee College of Music’s online program. Cassie began her studies with the piano at the age of three and soon found her love for writing music.

She has had numerous compositions commissioned for plays, commercials, apps, schools, visual artists, chamber groups, and films nationwide. As a film composer and orchestrator, she has worked for Terrence Malick, Martin Sheen, Hanan Townshend, Dragon Ball-Z’s composer Bruce Faulconer, and Funny or Die’s filmmakers.

In addition to writing music for film and media, Cassie has performed with Wycliffe Gordon in New York and has been invited to and attended the exclusive Grammy 2012, 2013, and 2015 awards. Cassie is also the founder and lead product developer for Picardy Learning, an interactive website for developing musicianship skills. She is currently working on Picardy Learning, various upcoming film projects, gigging, and teaching privately.
