SARAH STANCIK

Designer | Innovator | Experiencer


In the 1960s, Marshall McLuhan popularized the term “global village”, referring to the globalization that would result from new digital communication methods. His predictions proved to be true about the Internet today and continue to influence how people interact across the world. As technology continues to grow, I am fascinated by the future it holds in community growth.

With architecture as a lens, I see so much potential in using the power of this technology to create meaningful social changes. I am interested in bridging these digital and physical platforms of design to create meaningful user experiences at all scales.
